Founded in the year 1998 as an independent division of Sherman Group of Companies, Akiko Sherman Infotech Pvt. Ltd. has always been at the forefront of delivering best-in-class services to its clients with its sharp focus in the E-Governance space.

With extensive experience in IT Technology Solutions, Akiko Sherman Infotech has a rich portfolio of offerings for eGovernance and Enterprise sectors. In the e-Governance sector, the company has successfully delivered solutions for various central & state government departments and PSUs. We have always been at the forefront of delivering best-in-class services and solutions through our consulting-driven approach, which we believe is the biggest precursor to solving business problems of our clients and helping them grow.
